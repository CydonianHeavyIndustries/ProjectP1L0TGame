PROJECT P1L0T — GAMEPLAY ROADMAP (CANON)

====================================
HIGH-LEVEL IDENTITY
====================================
Genre:
Open-world, massively online, high-mobility FPS (PvE / PvP / PvPvE hybrid)

Core Fantasy:
Players are Pilots operating across hostile planets, supported by bonded Titans and mobile spaceship bases. The game blends fast parkour combat with MMO-scale social, economic, and faction systems.

Primary Inspirations:
- Titanfall (movement, Titans, pilot fantasy)
- Warframe (launcher, live-service shell, long-term progression)
- Tarkov (weapon customization, extraction tension)
- Destiny (shared world + safe hubs)
- Shadow of Mordor (Nemesis system)

====================================
WORLD STRUCTURE
====================================
Planet-Based MMO:
- The universe is split into planets.
- Each planet is a large open world with server instances/shards.
- New planets are added as the player base grows.
- Planets vary by:
  - Environment
  - Factions present
  - Resources
  - PvP intensity

Safe Zones / Hubs:
- Major cities and hubs are PvP-disabled safe zones.
- Used for:
  - Trading & market access
  - Social interaction
  - Clan management
  - Missions & contracts
  - Customization

====================================
SOCIAL STRUCTURE
====================================
Parties & Clans:
- Temporary parties for missions or roaming.
- Persistent clans with shared progression.
- Clan size affects:
  - Spaceship size
  - Personnel capacity
  - Titan transport capacity

Permissions:
- Spawn permissions (ships)
- Ship access levels
- Titan bay access

====================================
FACTIONS
====================================
- Players choose a faction or subfaction (lore-backed, placeholder early).
- Factions influence:
  - Reputation
  - PvP incentives
  - NPC hostility
  - Nemesis pool
- Faction switching is restricted.

====================================
GAMEPLAY MODES
====================================
PvE:
- Open-world PvE encounters.
- Loot-based progression.
- Resource gathering.
- Missions & contracts.
- Extraction-style risk/reward loops.

PvP:
- Open-world PvP zones.
- Faction-based or free-for-all depending on planet.
- PvP rewards:
  - Bonus XP
  - Currency
  - Reputation
  - Rare loot chances

PvPvE:
- Players and AI coexist.
- PvP can occur during PvE objectives.
- Increased risk over time for better rewards.
- Nemesis AI may appear.

====================================
SPACESHIP SYSTEM (CORE PILLAR)
====================================
Spaceship as Home/Base:
- Each player or clan owns a spaceship.
- Functions:
  - Spawn point
  - Mobile base
  - Social hub
  - Titan carrier

Rules:
- Ships are indestructible (can be disabled, not destroyed).
- Ships despawn when player logs off.
- Ships respawn at last location when player logs in.
- Players spawn inside their ship.
- Players may request permission to spawn in other ships.

Clan Ships:
- Larger clans unlock larger ships.
- More Titan bays and internal space.
- Heavy customization.

====================================
TITAN SYSTEM
====================================
Buddy Titan:
- Each player has a bonded Titan.
- Stored in spaceship in transport mode.
- Deployable during gameplay.

Titan Destruction:
- Titan destruction triggers a reconstruction cooldown.
- Titan respawns inside the spaceship.

Titan AI Learning:
- Titan AI learns player behavior.
- Adapts to combat style over time.
- Improved pilot–Titan synergy.
- Player names the Titan OS.
- Titan OS personality configurable.

====================================
CUSTOMIZATION SYSTEMS
====================================
Player Character:
- Full character creator.
- Cosmetic and identity focused.

Weapons:
- Deep, Tarkov-style weapon customization.
- Attachments, mods, trade-offs.
- Mechanical impact, not cosmetic-only.

Titans:
- Heavy visual and mechanical customization.
- Loadouts, modules, AI behavior tuning.

Spaceships:
- Modular interior expansion.
- Functional rooms.
- Visual identity reflects player or clan.

====================================
ECONOMY & MARKET
====================================
Economy:
- One or more in-game currencies.
- Resources gathered via PvE.
- Loot extraction loops.

Market:
- Player-to-player trading.
- Possibly regional markets per planet.
- Strong inflation control required.

====================================
MISSIONS & REWARDS
====================================
- Dynamic missions and contracts.
- Faction-aligned objectives.
- Rewards:
  - Currency
  - Gear
  - Reputation
  - Customization unlocks
- Difficulty scales with:
  - Group size
  - Zone danger
  - PvP risk

====================================
PROGRESSION
====================================
Skill Tree:
- Deep branching skill trees.
- Affects pilot abilities, Titan synergy, and utility.
- Avoid hard meta locks.
- Limited respec options.

====================================
NEMESIS SYSTEM
====================================
- Introduced during campaign/tutorial/onboarding.
- Player choices generate a personal Nemesis.
- Nemesis:
  - Belongs to opposing faction.
  - Remembers encounters.
  - Evolves over time.
  - Can invade PvE, PvPvE, and rare PvP scenarios.
- Creates long-term rivalries and emergent stories.

====================================
DEVELOPMENT PHASES (REALISTIC)
====================================
Phase 0 — Infrastructure:
- Launcher
- Menu shell
- Profile persistence

Phase 1 — Pilot Core:
- Movement perfection
- Weapon handling
- Minimal PvE enemies

Phase 2 — Shared World:
- One planet
- One hub city
- Limited PvE/PvP zones

Phase 3 — Spaceships & Titans:
- One ship type
- One Titan per player
- Basic deployment/respawn loop

Phase 4 — Economy & Missions:
- Loot system
- Trading
- Contracts

Phase 5 — Expansion:
- More planets
- More factions
- Clan ships
- Nemesis depth
- Skill trees

====================================
FINAL NOTE
====================================
Project P1L0T is a live-service platform, not a single game mode.
Systems must be built in layers.
Inputs, identity, and core fantasy are locked early.
Everything else evolves around that foundation.
